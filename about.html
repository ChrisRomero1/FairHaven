Our Story

Grounded in Experience.
Elevated in Service.

Fairhaven Property Solutions was founded on a simple belief: property management should be professional, responsive, and genuinely focused on client success. We're building something different: boutique in our attention, institutional in our capability, and relentless in our commitment to results.

Why Fairhaven

Built Different.
By Design.

Most property management companies were built to scale volume. We were built to deliver results.

After years in the industry, we saw a pattern: owners were underserved, communication was reactive, and management felt transactional. Property owners and investors deserved a partner who understood their goals, not just their rent roll.

Fairhaven was built to be that partner.

We combine deep local market knowledge with modern technology, proactive communication, and an uncompromising commitment to performance. Every decision we make is grounded in one question: Is this good for the asset and the owner?

We operate across single family homes, multifamily communities, and HOA associations throughout the Southeast. Our clients choose us not because we're the biggest, but because we're the most invested.

That's the Fairhaven difference.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Transparency

We build trust through clarity and straight-up honesty. No hidden agendas, no surprises—just open communication and full visibility at every step. Our clients always know where things stand.

Excellence

Mediocrity doesn't live here. We hold every property, every system, and every client experience to a higher standard because real value doesn't come from doing the minimum. It comes from refusing to settle for it.

Accountability

Every promise, every process, every result is on us. We don't point fingers or make excuses. We own the outcome, full stop, and we hold ourselves and our partners to that same standard without exception.

Respect

People first, always. We treat owners, residents, vendors, and our team with fairness and dignity because strong relationships and thriving communities don't happen by accident. They're built intentionally, one interaction at a time.

Growth

We are invested in the success of our clients, our team, and our company. We pursue continuous improvement, embrace new ideas, and push ourselves to deliver more value today than we did yesterday.

Integrity

Everything we do is grounded in doing the right thing, even when it's inconvenient, even when no one is watching. Our word is our bond and our reputation is our most valuable asset. We protect both without compromise.

Leadership

Meet the Founder

Coya S.

Principal Broker

Coya is the Founder and Principal Broker of Fairhaven Property Solutions, a boutique property management firm built on a clear standard: asset-focused management that delivers measurable results.

With a background spanning property management, real estate brokerage, and investment strategy, she brings a well-rounded, operator-level perspective to every asset under her care. Her approach is grounded in systems, data, and execution, with a focus on performance, efficiency, and long-term value.

Coya leads with a high level of accountability and clarity. She believes property management should go beyond basic oversight and function as a true partnership. That means proactive communication, strategic decision-making, and consistent follow-through that investors can rely on.

Based in Ringgold, Georgia, and serving clients across the Southeast and beyond, she is building Fairhaven Property Solutions into a trusted, results-driven firm known for doing things the right way, not the easy way.
