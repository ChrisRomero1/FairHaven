Our Story

Grounded in Experience.
Elevated in Service.

Fairhaven Property Solutions was founded on a simple belief: property management should be professional, responsive, and genuinely focused on client success. We're building something different—boutique service with institutional capability.

Why Fairhaven

Built Different.
By Design.

After years in the industry, we saw a gap. Property owners and investors deserved better than cookie-cutter management or faceless corporate service. They deserved a partner who understood their goals and had the systems to deliver.

Fairhaven was created to fill that gap. We combine deep local market knowledge with modern technology, responsive communication, and a genuine commitment to our clients' success.

Today, we manage approximately 180 doors and growing—each one treated with the attention it deserves. From multifamily investors to HOA boards, our clients choose us because we deliver results with integrity.

Leadership

Meet the Principal

Coya S.

Principal Broker

Coya founded Fairhaven Property Solutions with a vision to create a different kind of property management company—one that combines professional expertise with genuine care for clients and communities.

With extensive experience in real estate investment, property management, and brokerage, Coya brings a comprehensive understanding of what it takes to succeed in today's market. Her approach is grounded in data, elevated by relationships, and always focused on delivering measurable results.

Under Coya's leadership, Fairhaven has grown to manage approximately 180 doors while maintaining the boutique service standards that set the company apart. She believes that every property deserves professional management, and every client deserves a partner who truly understands their goals.

When she's not working with clients or analyzing market trends, Coya is passionate about building community—both within Fairhaven and in the neighborhoods the company serves.
