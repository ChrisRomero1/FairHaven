1. Program Overview

Fairhaven Property Solutions LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates an SMS messaging program to facilitate timely and efficient communication with prospective tenants, current residents, property owners, and individuals who have submitted inquiries through our website or direct channels.

This program is designed to support our property management and real estate brokerage services, not for promotional marketing or third-party advertising.

Once you opt in, you may receive SMS messages related to any of the following:

Leasing and Prospective Tenants

  • Rental inquiry responses and property information

  • Application status updates
  • Lease signing reminders and document requests

Current Residents

  • Maintenance request updates and scheduling
  • Rent reminders and payment confirmations

Property Owners and Clients

Upon receipt of a valid STOP request, we will send a one-time confirmation message and then cease sending SMS messages to your number. If you later wish to re-enroll, you may do so by opting in again through any of the methods described in Section 3.

Opting out of SMS does not affect other communications such as email or phone calls that may be required for active services.
